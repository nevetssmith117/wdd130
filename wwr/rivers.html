River Adventures: Unleash the Thrill

Embark on a journey through America's most iconic riversâ€”from Idaho's Salmon River to Utah's Desolation and Gray Canyon, and the legendary Grand Canyon in Arizona. Experience the thrill of whitewater rapids, encounter rich wildlife, and explore historic sites on these unforgettable adventures.

Salmon River

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho: Journey through the "River of No Return" in the Frank Church Wilderness, where its untouched beauty captivates many. Witness elk, deer, bears, and bighorn sheep in their natural habitat, alongside majestic ponderosa pines. Enjoy a range of rapids from class I-IV on this undammed river, with conditions varying from cold, fast waters in early summer to warmer, slower flows ideal for family swims in late summer.

Gray Canyon

Explore the Green River as it carves through Utah's stark landscapes, from Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid. Renowned for its high cliffs, desert beauty, and thrilling rapids, this 84-mile journey offers a blend of spectacular views, geological wonders, and traces of human history. Rated Class II/III, the trip through Desolation Canyon, a National Landmark, reveals a story of natural grandeur and historical significance over an immersive six-day adventure.

Grand Canyon

Grand Canyon, Arizona: Experience the ultimate river adventure in the Grand Canyon, the pinnacle of American canyons. Offering a mix of world-class whitewater rapids and stunning scenery, this trip takes you through ancient ruins and dramatic side canyons. With a unique rating scale from I to X, the Grand Canyon provides a diverse range of rapids to suit all levels of adventure seekers.
