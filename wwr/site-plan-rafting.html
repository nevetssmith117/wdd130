Dry Oar Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Dry Oar site plan is to build revenue for the business by creating an enticing and well-managed adventure destination. The plan aims to design an exhilarating white water rafting experience that attracts a wide range of adventure seekers, leading to increased visitor numbers and revenue generation. Prioritizing safety and sustainability, the site plan seeks to provide an unforgettable rafting adventure that encourages repeat visits and positive word-of-mouth promotion. By strategically incorporating amenities, marketing initiatives, and customer-focused services, the goal is to establish the white water rafting site as a profitable venture, contributing to the financial success and growth of the business.

Audience

The target customers for Dry Oar are adventure enthusiasts aged 18 to 40, seeking an adrenaline-packed white water rafting experience. These young professionals and college students are interested in outdoor activities and have a moderate to above-average disposable income. They want a memorable adventure that combines excitement with safety, with a focus on hassle-free booking and facilities that enhance their overall experience. The needs not fully met include a seamless booking process and a sense of community during the adventure. Dry Oar aims to address these needs by providing high-quality rafting services, user-friendly mobile-optimized booking options, and opportunities for participants to connect and share their adventure stories. The target audience is expected to primarily access the website through mobile devices, ensuring a convenient and engaging user experience.
